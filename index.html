About The Festival

Harmony Fest is an annual celebration of music, art, and community that brings together people from all walks of life. Since its inception in 2015, our festival has grown to become one of the most anticipated events of the summer.

This year, we're taking things to the next level with three days of incredible performances across four stages, immersive art installations, gourmet food vendors, and interactive workshops.

Whether you're a music enthusiast, art lover, or simply looking for a memorable weekend, Harmony Fest has something for everyone. Join us for an unforgettable experience that celebrates creativity, connection, and the power of music.
